Language Translation

English

English is a widely spoken global language used for communication, education, business, and media. It is known for its rich vocabulary and flexibility across cultures and regions.

Chinese

Chinese is a tonal language spoken by over a billion people, primarily in China. It has several dialects, with Mandarin being the most widely used, and it uses characters instead of an alphabet.

Portuguese

Portuguese is a Romance language spoken mainly in Portugal and Brazil. It uses the Latin alphabet and has similarities with Spanish, but distinct pronunciation and vocabulary.

Japanese

Japanese is the official language of Japan, using three scripts: Hiragana, Katakana, and Kanji. It has a unique grammar structure and is not directly related to Chinese, though it uses many Chinese characters.

French

French is a Romance language spoken in France and many parts of the world. It uses the Latin alphabet and is known for its elegant pronunciation and grammar with gendered nouns.

German

German is a West Germanic language spoken mainly in Germany, Austria, and Switzerland. It uses the Latin alphabet and is known for its compound words and strict grammar rules.

Italian

Italian is a Romance language spoken mainly in Italy and parts of Switzerland. It uses the Latin alphabet and is known for its melodic sound and close connection to Latin.

Korean

Korean is the official language of South and North Korea, written using the unique Hangul script. It has a subject-object-verb sentence structure and is known for its honorifics and speech levels.
